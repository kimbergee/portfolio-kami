Hello. My name is Kimber and I'm a Full Stack Web Developer based in Boston. Before becoming a developer, I was working in the digital marketing space. I worked with a small tech start up for a while, where I worked closely with the developers. Seeing how and what they did to build the products that I was marketing, made me realize that I wanted to do that as well- solve problems, make things work, and create things that people like to use. In my experience, there is often a disconnect between marketers and programmers because they have difficulty understanding each other. With my experience in both spaces, I try to build a bridge between the two and always keep a marketing mindset when developing apps. I have worked with building both backend & frontend projects, but enjoy frontend development & design the most.

When I'm not coding, I can most likely be found playing or taking photos of my two rescue pups, Luna and Arnold (@lunathetoona). I am also a freelance writer and you can find some of my articles on The Huffington Post, BarkPost, and The Dodo.

If you want to talk to me more, you can send me a message at hello@kimbergee.com.
