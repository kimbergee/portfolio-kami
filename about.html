Hello. My name is Kimber and I'm a Full Stack Web Developer based in Boston, MA. Before becoming a developer, I worked on digital marketing solutions for a number of organizations and most recently a small data analytics tech startup.

Throughout my career, I've collaborated closely with development teams and in doing so, became fascinated with the process by which programmers created useful products that people loved using. I also realized that there were often inefficiencies between the work of marketing teams and programmers due to the lack of a common language. As a result, I'm now working to bridge this gap, approaching all of my development projects with a marketing and user-focused mindset. I have experience working on both front & backend projects, and find a particular enjoyment in the aspects of frontend development and design.

When I'm not coding, I can most likely be found playing with or taking photos of my two rescue pups, Luna and Arnold (@lunathetoona). I am also a freelance writer and you can find some of my articles on The Huffington Post, BarkPost, and The Dodo.

If you want to talk to me more, you can send me a message at hello@kimbergee.com.
